OUR GAMES...

Gaming Zone is an absolute platform that takes us to childhood nostalgia. It includes the basic games which we used to play manually as a child. So, this gaming platform gives an opportunity to rejoice in that same pleasure with technology. This platform includes 4 games:

TIC TAC TOE:

A game for two players who take turns marking the spaces in a three- by-three grid with X or O The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner. It is a solved game, with a forced draw assuming the best play from both players.

ROCK PAPER SCISSORS:

It has three possible outcomes: a draw, a win, or a loss. A player who decides to play rock will beat another player who has chosen scissors but will lose to one who has played paper; a play of paper will lose to a play of scissors. If both players choose the same shape, the game is tied. SURF A WAY: Swipe left and right to switch lanes to avoid obstacles/blocks. If you fail to escape the obstacles or blocks, you lose.

ROLLING DICE:

As one player rolls the dice, the numbers that one gets on the dice get added until a 'one' on the dice makes the sum equal to zero. The player can pass the chance to another player anytime during the game. As soon as the player gets a 'one' on the dice, his sum becomes zero and the chance is automatically passed over to the next player. The player who reaches the set limit of sum first is the winner.
